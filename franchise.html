Frequently Asked Questions

What sets Pizza World apart? +

Pizza World combines the world's finest gourmet pizzas with subs, calzones, salads, wings and traditional pizzas — all available for quick delivery or dine-in. We use only the freshest ingredients, real cheese, homemade sauce, and freshly baked dough. The entire system is designed for simplicity and customer convenience. We're changing the way the world thinks about pizza.

What are the space requirements? +

Delivery-focused units require 1,100 to 1,300 sq ft. Delivery plus sit-down restaurant units can go up to 2,400 sq ft. Typically located in strip centers (in-line or end caps) with good signage visibility, strong traffic counts, and proximity to residential areas, schools, and other customer sources.

What training is provided? +

It starts with our virtual training program that franchisees complete before ever stepping into a store. Then two weeks of hands-on training at a designated Pizza World location covering operations, computer systems, food preparation, delivery, daily management, and more. The virtual training platform stays available after opening — every new employee you hire goes through it too. Plus you get in-store assistance during your grand opening and ongoing support.

How long does it take to open? +

Construction typically takes 90 to 120 days, depending on location, store style, and size. Pizza World provides site and plan approval, construction and equipment specifications, design specifications, and approved supplier lists to streamline the process.

Is financing available? +

Yes — Pizza World is SBA approved, which means qualified candidates can access Small Business Administration financing through participating banks. You must demonstrate the financial ability to start, build, and manage the business.

What ongoing fees are there? +

Royalty: 2.75% of net sales (the industry's lowest). Marketing: franchisees are required to spend 2% of net sales on marketing, but you decide how to spend it — we don't collect it. That's it — no hidden fees, no surprise charges. The franchise agreement runs for 10 years with a 10-year renewal option.
